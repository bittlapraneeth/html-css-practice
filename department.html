Department And courses

The degree college is organized into three primary study areas offering several specializations.

  • Department of Management Studies
    BBA:     Bachelor of Business Administration (General). BBA (Business Analytics): Focused on data-driven decision-making. BBA (Business Intelligence & Analytics): Advanced data and intelligence studies.
  • Department of Commerce
    B.Com (Honors):     A more intensive commerce program. B.Com (Computer Applications): Blending commerce with essential programming skills. B.Com (Business Analytics): Commerce integrated with analytical tools.
  • Department of Science & Computer Applications
    BCA:     Bachelor of Computer Applications. B.Sc (Computer Science): MSCS (Maths, Statistics, Computer Science). B.Sc (Data Science): Specialized study in statistical data analysis. B.Sc (AI & ML): Focused on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. B.Sc (Hons) in Computer Science: A 4-year professional science degree
