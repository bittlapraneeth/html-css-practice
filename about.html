Histroy of the College

Siva Sivani Degree College (SSDC) in Hyderabad was established in 2002 by the visionary Sri. S.P. Sampathy, who founded the institution as part of the larger Siva Sivani Group of Institutions, founded in 1961 with a vision for quality education. Affiliated with Osmania University, SSDC offers undergraduate programs like BBA, B.Com, and B.Sc, focusing on holistic development and bridging classroom learning with real-world application, carrying forward the group's legacy of excellence in education.

Key Aspects

Key Accreditations: Over the years, the institute has earned several prestigious certifications: NAAC: It was the first business school in the region to be accredited by NAAC. NBA: Its flagship PGDM programs are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. International Recognition: It holds accreditation from the South Asian Quality Assurance System (SAQS) and is a member of the AACSB (Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business).
